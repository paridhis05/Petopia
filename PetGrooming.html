Frequently Asked Questions

What if I want the same groomer each time?
Petsfolio strives its best to accommodate customized requirements like this as many pet parents prefer the same groomer each time. This indeed works best when it comes to building a positive relationship between the pet parent, groomer and pet especially if your pet gets nervous while grooming.
Why should I choose Petsfolio over other grooming service providers?
Petsfolio focuses on offering the finest grooming experience possible with the most quality-focused yet affordable pet grooming services. All the pet groomers are trained to provide exceptional attention and care for pets of all sizes and shapes with the help of the most advanced and highest quality equipment. As a pet parent whatever you need your pet to be whether bathed, styled, trimmed, Petsfolio pet groomer services have got you covered.
What are the benefits of dog grooming?
Regular baths, brushing, dental hygiene,hair-cutting, nail-cutting and more are essential to the health of the skin andcoat of your pet. Where regular baths prevent bad odour by loosening dirt,other grooming services enhance a sense of overall wellness in your pet.
Can professional pet groomers groom my dog to the breed standard?
When you choose to rely on pet grooming services offered by a reputed firm like Petsfoilio, you can rest assured that the pet groomers will be familiar with most of the breed standards. Moreover, regular grooming also helps you maintain the grooming to the breed standard.
Is it necessary to hire professional groomers for my pet grooming?
Though it is not necessary a hectic lifestyle should not be a reason for you to put up with a stinky pet. The rise of professional pet grooming services indicates that the majority of pet parents are now choosing to rely on professional pet grooming services. The experienced groomers trained in advanced grooming sessions such as bathing, brushing, nail polishing, teeth polishing and more help your pet to lead a healthy and pleasant life.
