Frequently Asked Questions

What Are Some Essential Nutrients That Should Be Included in My Pet’s Diet?
Your pet’s diet should include essential nutrients like proteins, fats, carbohydrates, vitamins, and minerals to ensure their optimal health. High-quality pet foods from reputed brands like Royal Canin, Farmina, Hills Pet, and Orijen are formulated to meet these nutritional needs.
When Should I Take My Pet for a Vet Check-Up?
Routine vet visits are essential for monitoring your pet’s health. Puppies and kittens should have monthly check-ups, while healthy adult pets should see a vet at least once a year. Senior pets may require more frequent visits.
What Are the Signs of Common Health Issues in Pets?
Some common signs of health problems include loss of appetite, vomiting, diarrhea, lethargy, excessive scratching, and difficulty breathing. If you notice any of these symptoms, consult a veterinarian immediately.
What Vaccinations Does My Pet Need?
Vaccinations protect pets from various diseases. Core vaccines for dogs include rabies, distemper, parvovirus, and hepatitis. For cats, essential vaccines include rabies, feline calicivirus, and feline herpesvirus. Your vet will provide a vaccination schedule suited for your pet.
What Are the Benefits of Pet Insurance?
Pet insurance helps cover medical expenses in case of accidents, illnesses, and routine health check-ups. This ensures your pet gets the best medical care without financial strain.
Do You Offer 24/7 Emergency Veterinary Services?
Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency vet services for critical conditions. In case of an emergency, contact our helpline immediately.
