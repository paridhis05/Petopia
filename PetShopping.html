Which Food Should I Feed My Cats, Dry Foods or Gravy Foods?
Both dry foods and gravy foods have their own benefits and both of them are nutritionally wholesome for felines. However, as cats are generally picky eaters, gravy foods may tempt them more than dry cat foods. At PetsWorld, you can check out some of the top-notch gravy food brands like Royal Canin, Whiskas, Me-O, Bellotta, Sheba etc. Gravy foods not only elevate the overall health of your cats, but also keep them thoroughly hydrated in the scorching heat of the summers.
