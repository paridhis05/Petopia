Frequently Asked Questions

How do you select your dog walkers?
For every dog walker, we do a thorough background check before getting that person onboard in our dog walking agency. We get only those people onboard who are passionate about animals specially dogs. We make sure that these people enjoy what they do and can handle any breed of dog with care and love. So yes we assure you that your dog is absolutely in safe hands.
How does Petopia pet-sitting work?
When you book our pet-sitting service, a qualified pet sitter will visit your home at scheduled times. They will ensure your pet is fed, given fresh water, and provided with ample playtime and exercise.
Can I request additional services for my pet during the pet-sitting sessions?
Certainly! We offer several add-on services that you can request for your pet, such as extended playtime, additional walks, grooming sessions, administering medications, and basic training reinforcement. When booking, let us know your requirements, and we’ll ensure your pet’s needs are met.
What happens if my pet has special needs or requires medication?
If your pet has special needs or requires medication, it’s important to communicate these details to the host beforehand. Provide clear medication administration instructions and inform the host about your pet’s specific care requirements.
What are the payment options for Pawspace Home Pet Walking and Sitting Services?
We offer various payment options, including online payments, credit/debit cards, and other secure payment methods. You’ll receive detailed information about the payment process when booking.
Are Dog walks with or without leash?
We take your dog for a walk with leash only unless you specifically mention not to. However, it will be at owner’s risk, if dogs off leash are taken for the walk.
